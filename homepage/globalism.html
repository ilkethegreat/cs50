Why Globalism
Globally Enriching
Unlike prevailing misperception, the globalism carries benefits for all nations. The increasing economical collaborations accelerates the growth of the world economy and the technology, which increases the prosperity everywhere. This can be observed from the recent decades, increasing global trade has caused the productive countries to get even more prosperous, meanwhile the emerging countries received the benefits of the technology that the productive countries create, which caused them to get more prosperous, which caused the productive countries to be able to trade with those countries even more. As can be seen, the globalism creates a self-fulfilling dynamic that creates value for all nations.
A Greater Vision
Humans get divided if they look down, but united if they look up. Focusing on globalism not only will enrich every nation, but also will give us a greater vision. Instead of getting choked among everyday politics and issues, while getting more prosperous, we will have something that is worth of our dignity and nobility as humans, a greater place to conquer, a greater vision that suites to our capabilities infinitely more: The universe and behind it.
Unification
As the most advanced creatures on Earth, we should be reaching to our greatest potential through the benefits non-utilitarian collaboration gives. That devotion to our collaboration will increasingly unify us and slowly take away our lower-animal selves.
